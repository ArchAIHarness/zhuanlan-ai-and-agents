# Cover: 别再找 Prompt 模板了

## Style
Hand-drawn educational framework diagram style on warm cream paper background (#F5F0E8). Black sketch lines, soft pastel color blocks in orange, green, and blue. Handwritten Chinese text labels. Simple line art icons. No dark backgrounds, no 3D, no realistic human faces.

## Composition
Wide 16:9 horizontal layout, generous whitespace (40-60%).

**Left side**: A simple sketch-style human figure (silhouette, no face detail) sitting at a desk, holding a pen, looking at a document. Drawn in thin black sketch lines.

**Center**: A large document/contract floating between the human and the AI. The contract has sketch lines representing text clauses, with a prominent "契约" (contract) label in handwritten Chinese. A pen and a stamp/seal are next to it. Soft pastel orange blocks highlight the contract.

**Right side**: A simple sketch-style AI/robot icon (minimal, no face detail) represented by a monitor or abstract AI symbol, with gentle green and blue pastel accents. The AI is receiving the contract.

**Connecting element**: Arrows in sketch style flowing from human → contract → AI, with a small label "任务契约" connecting them.

## Title text
"别再找 Prompt 模板了" - large, centered at the top, handwritten Chinese style, dark brown/black ink color.

## Subtitle text (smaller, bottom area)
"提示词的本质，是你和 AI 的任务契约" - smaller handwritten Chinese, positioned below the title.

## Colors
- Background: #F5F0E8 (warm cream)
- Lines: #2C2C2C (near-black sketch)
- Pastels: #E8A87C (warm orange), #85BAA1 (sage green), #7BA7C9 (soft blue)
- Title: #2C1810 (dark brown ink)
