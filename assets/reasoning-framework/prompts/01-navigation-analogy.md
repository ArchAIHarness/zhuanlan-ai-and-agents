# 01 导航与自主推理的类比

## Style
Hand-drawn educational framework diagram on warm cream paper (#F5F0E8). Black sketch lines. Soft pastel blocks in orange, green, blue. Handwritten Chinese labels. Simple line icons.

## Content
A side-by-side comparison illustration showing the analogy between a navigation app and AI autonomous reasoning.

**Left side (导航 - Navigation App)**: A simple smartphone screen sketch showing a map with a route. Below it, a flow in sketch style: 输入目的地 → 规划路线 → 按导航行驶 → 遇到封路 → 重新规划. Small icons: pin icon, road icon, detour sign.

**Right side (AI 自主推理)**: A simple computer/robot sketch. Below it, a matching flow: 接收任务 → 拆解步骤 → 执行 → 发现错误 → 自我修正. Small icons: document, checklist, gear icon.

**Center**: A large double-headed arrow connecting both sides, labeled "结构一致" in handwritten Chinese.

**Bottom**: A small label: "所有自主推理框架，本质都是让AI按流程走"

Use simple line art, no realistic people or faces.
