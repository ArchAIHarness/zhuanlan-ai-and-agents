# Cover: 看懂 AI 与智能体 (Column Cover)

## Style
Hand-drawn educational framework diagram style on warm cream paper background (#F5F0E8). Black sketch lines, soft pastel color blocks in orange, green, and blue. Handwritten Chinese text labels. Simple line art icons. No dark backgrounds, no 3D, no realistic human faces. Consistent with the column's article illustration style.

## Composition
Wide 16:9 horizontal layout, generous whitespace (40-60%).

**Background**: Warm cream paper texture (#F5F0E8), with subtle hand-drawn border decorations in the corners.

**Center visual**: A bridge or pathway connecting two sides:
- **Left side**: A simple sketch silhouette of a person (普通用户/普通人) standing on a foundation labeled "直觉理解" (intuitive understanding) in handwritten Chinese. Above them float simple icons representing concepts from the early articles: a brain (模型), a robot arm (Agent), a scroll (上下文/Token), a toolbox (Tool/Skill/MCP).
- **Right side**: A simple sketch of an engineer/architect at a desk with a computer, standing on a foundation labeled "工程落地" (engineering practice). Above them float icons: a gear (自主推理), a database (记忆/知识库), connected nodes (多Agent协作), a blueprint (架构设计).
- **Center bridge**: A sketch-style bridge or staircase connecting left and right, labeled "从认知到实践" (from understanding to practice) in handwritten Chinese. Small arrows flow along the bridge from left to right.

**Top area**: The column title "看懂 AI 与智能体" in large handwritten Chinese, dark brown ink color, centered.

**Bottom area**: A subtle banner ribbon with ArchAIHarness brand mark and the motto "人立法，AI 执行，体系审计" in smaller handwritten Chinese.

**Around the edges**: Scattered subtle sketch elements representing the 7 published article themes: a sparkle icon, a document icon, a memory icon, arranged organically.

## Title text
