# Cover: 自主推理框架——AI 怎么自己拆任务、自己修正

## Style
Hand-drawn educational framework diagram on warm cream paper (#F5F0E8). Black sketch lines. Soft pastel blocks in orange, green, blue. Handwritten Chinese labels. Simple line icons. No dark backgrounds, no 3D, no realistic faces. Consistent with column style.

## Composition
Wide 16:9 horizontal layout, generous whitespace.

Center visual: A sketch-style circular ReAct loop (Think → Act → Observe → back to Think) drawn with black sketch lines. The loop is highlighted with soft pastel colored blocks (blue for Think, orange for Act, green for Observe). Small icons next to each step: lightbulb, gear, magnifying glass, circular arrow.

Left side: A simple sketch of a human figure (silhouette, no face) pointing at the loop.

Right side: A simple computer/robot icon receiving the loop.

Arrows flow from human → loop → robot/AI, representing "humans design the framework, AI executes it."

Top: Large handwritten Chinese title "自主推理框架" in dark brown ink, centered.

Bottom: Smaller handwritten Chinese subtitle "AI怎么自己拆任务、自己修正" and a subtle "ArchAIHarness" watermark in bottom right.

## Colors
- Background: #F5F0E8 warm cream
- Lines: #2C2C2C near-black
- Pastels: #E8A87C (warm orange), #85BAA1 (sage green), #7BA7C9 (soft blue)
- Title: #2C1810 dark brown
