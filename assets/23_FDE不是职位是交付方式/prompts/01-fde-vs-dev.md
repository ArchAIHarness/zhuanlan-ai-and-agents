# 01: FDE vs 驻场开发

## Style
Hand-drawn educational framework diagram on warm cream paper (#F5F0E8). Black sketch lines (#2C2C2C), soft pastel blocks. No dark backgrounds, no 3D, no realistic faces.

## Composition
Square 1:1. Two columns side by side with a large "VS" in the center. Left: a person at a desk receiving a document labeled "需求", caption "驻场开发：需求定了你去实现". Right: a person exploring/questioning, caption "FDE：你去发现客户真正的问题". Color: left blue (#7BA7C9), right orange (#E8A87C).
