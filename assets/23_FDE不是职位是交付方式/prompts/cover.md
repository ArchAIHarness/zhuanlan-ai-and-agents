# Cover: FDE 不是职位是交付方式

## Style
Hand-drawn educational framework diagram style on warm cream paper background (#F5F0E8). Black sketch lines (#2C2C2C), soft pastel color blocks in orange (#E8A87C), sage green (#85BAA1), soft blue (#7BA7C9). Handwritten Chinese text labels. Simple line art icons. No dark backgrounds, no 3D, no realistic human faces.

## Composition
16:9 wide layout. Three columns connected by arrows flowing left to right.

**Left column (传统交付)**: A simple person silhouette labeled "驻场开发" in handwritten Chinese. Below them: a desk with a computer, a document marked "需求" (requirements). Color block: soft blue (#7BA7C9) background.

**Center column (AI 执行)**: A cloud/robot icon representing AI, with small icons around it: code bracket, test checkmark, deploy rocket. Labeled "AI 执行" in handwritten Chinese. Below: brief text "代码/测试/部署". Color block: sage green (#85BAA1) background.

**Right column (FDE 交付)**: A person silhouette standing on a foundation labeled "表达+业务架构+沉淀". Above them: connected icons - chat bubble (沟通), gear (业务), puzzle (对接), growth arrow (中台). Labeled "FDE 落地" in handwritten Chinese. Color block: warm orange (#E8A87C) background.

**Arrows between columns**: Three sketch arrows flowing left to center, center to right. Arrow from center to right is thicker/bolder, emphasizing the shift.

**Title area**: Top of image, large handwritten Chinese: "FDE 不是职位，是交付方式"

**Bottom strip**: Small text: "AI 写代码的能力越强，把 AI 接到业务里的人就越值钱"
